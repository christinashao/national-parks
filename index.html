Native Americans
  • Archeologists believe the Yosemite area has been inhabited for 8,000 years.
  • The Southern Miwok people were the last native group to live in the area before non-native contact.
  • Native Americans who lived in Yosemite Valley called themselves Ahwahneechee.
  • Yosemite Valley was called Ahwahnee, which meant “gaping mouth-like place.”
